Emily (Em) Coombes

feminist sociologist researching networked movements

Currently, I’m a PhD Candidate in Sociology at University of Nevada, Las Vegas graduating in May 2025. My name is listed as Emily on most of my work. Some projects, papers, and presentations may be under the name Em instead.
